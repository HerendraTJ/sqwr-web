The Hudson Valley Hackerspace

Start Making

The Mid-Hudson Valley needs a place for hackers and makers to share tools, space, knowledge, and skills.

squidwrench has been sharing knowledge and ideas since January 2010. We've had expos and workshops, and random get togethers. In 2013 we teamed up with Studio 81 to provide the space and greater access to tools.

Whether your a programmer, and electonics whiz, a graphic designer, or a sculptor with an interest in tech, you probably have something to share. Check out our events and our photos to get an idea of what we already do, or come hang out at one of our Weekly Doings to see what other cool things we could do together.

Our Events

Weekly Doing

Got a project? Want to find out what Squidwrench is all about? Our Weekly Doings are a time for working on your project and general socializing.

Weekly Doings are open to members and non-members. Come hang out, see the shop, and talk to other hackers and makers.

Electronics Workshops

Whether you're a seasoned vet or squishy pink newbie, we've got the workshop for you. We have monthly learn-to-solder workshops, and several experienced folks to share war stories with.

Blacksmithing

We haven't actually done this, yet. But we will if there's interest! If this is something you want to do, come on out and let us know.

Pencil Sharpening

We've been voted the best pencil sharpening service for 10 consecutive years. If you have a pencil that feels dull, we'll get it sharp!
